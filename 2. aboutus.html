Our Story
7 years ago, Digital Espresso was born out of the passion of young Tristan to allow Filipinos to taste what coffee should really taste like.
Our Beans
Our Beans are sourced from secret caves of the Himalayan mountains. Only 2 beings have been to the exact location of these magic beans: Tristan and Chowder (his beloved Chowchow)
Our Craft
Tristan himself grinds the beans every morning with much passion and love. As his famous quote says, "Coffee isn't everything; it's the only thing."
